03 · The Plan

Four pillars. Specific tactics inside each.

The JD breaks this role into four weighted areas. Expand any pillar to see the named, concrete tactics I'd execute inside it — not theory, not direction, actual work.

The biggest lift happens here. The goal is building an integrated system where every project milestone, safety win, and culture moment becomes a traceable, compounding asset — not a one-off post.

Vertical SEO Content Hub
12–16 pillar articles targeting searches like "electrical contractor healthcare Texas" and "BIM-ready electrical subcontractor." Quarterly publishing cadence. Built for Google and answer engines (AEO/GEO).
Website Architecture Overhaul
Restructure around verticals — healthcare, datacenters, stadiums, industrial. Each gets a landing page with project proof, capability overview, and a clear BD inquiry path. Separate careers entry point.
LinkedIn Editorial Engine
Weekly cadence: project milestones Mondays, safety/process Wednesdays, culture spotlights Fridays. Channel-native — not repurposed press releases. Target: 3× organic reach within 6 months.
Paid Media with Real Attribution
UTM-tagged campaigns per vertical and per market. Retargeting for BD audiences. Separate recruiting paid strategy on LinkedIn and trades-specific platforms. Monthly ROAS reporting to leadership.
BD Pursuit Asset System
Templated one-pagers per vertical, pre-populated with project proof and safety metrics. A shared library so every RFQ/RFP response pulls from approved creative — not a scramble the week of submission.
Email Marketing Infrastructure
Quarterly owner/GC newsletter. Separate recruiting drip for candidate pipeline. CRM-connected with open rates, click-through, and conversion tracked and reported monthly.

"Wired For Excellence" is a strong platform — it just needs a consistent visual system built around it. I'd partner with the Marketing Director to evolve brand direction, then enforce standards across every deliverable. I bring a full in-house production kit so on-site storytelling doesn't wait on outside vendors.

Living Brand Standards Document
Typography, photography style, color usage, tone of voice, and social templates. Updated quarterly. Shared with every vendor and new hire — not a PDF that lives on a shelf.
On-Site Visual Production Pipeline
Scheduled quarterly jobsite visits across active markets. Photography and short-form video per visit. Build a reusable asset library so the team is never pulling from the same 12 photos.
Project Case Study System
Templated format: challenge, crew, timeline, safety record, result. Three formats per project — longform web, one-page PDF for BD, social-native short. Starting with DKR, Kalahari, NBU.
Collateral Template Library
Pre-approved InDesign templates for proposals, capability statements, and vertical one-pagers. Brand consistency without the team touching every print piece individually.

30% of this role is people — and that shows up in the JD. I'd invest in workflow clarity first, because most team dysfunction comes from unclear intake and undefined priorities, not from talent. This is the operating rhythm I built at Overland across architects, partners, and clients.

Marketing Intake System
Single intake form and triage process for requests from BD, Operations, HR, and Safety. Every request gets a priority tier, an owner, a deadline, and a QC checkpoint. No more reactive fire drills.
Weekly 1:1 Coaching Cadence
Structured 30-minute 1:1s with each direct report. Agenda: blockers, priorities, skill development, feedback. Quarterly development plans tied to individual goals — not just annual reviews.
Quality Control Checklist System
Deliverable-specific QC checklist for each output type — social, press release, proposal collateral, case study, email. Nothing ships without a second set of eyes.
Budget Visibility & Input
Monthly budget tracking shared with the Marketing Director. Spend mapped to campaigns with actual vs. planned variance noted. Input into annual planning based on prior quarter ROI.
Employee Advocacy Program
Structured program with easy-to-share content and branded templates for 10–15 volunteer ambassadors. Starts as a pilot, scales to the broader team. ESOP owners should act like owners.
Recruiting & Onboarding Support
Job descriptions that reflect brand voice, a careers page that converts, and onboarding materials that reinforce Alterman culture from day one. Partner with HR on all of it.

Marketing at a 500-person firm can't run on vibes. Everything gets measured — and the measurement answers one real question: where is the next dollar or hour best spent?

Quarterly KPI Dashboard
Web traffic, SEO rankings, social engagement, paid ROAS, email performance, proposal-to-win rate, cost-per-application. Shared with leadership quarterly. Built in Looker Studio.
Channel Monthly Reports
One-page monthly summary per active channel: what we ran, what the numbers were, what changes next month. Written for a non-marketer — leadership shouldn't need a decoder ring.
Campaign Attribution Tracking
Every URL is UTM-tagged. Every BD asset has a source code. Every paid ad is tied to a conversion event. When a pursuit comes in, we know if marketing touched it.
Marketing Asset Library
Tagged, searchable, version-controlled library of every approved asset. Accessible to BD without routing through the marketing team. Updated as deliverables ship.
